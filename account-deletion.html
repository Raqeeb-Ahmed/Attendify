Attendify Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Attendify account and associated data, you may request account deletion using the method below.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion directly from within the Attendify mobile application or by contacting our support team.

Email: support@attendify.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Attendance history
  • Check-in and Check-out records
  • Stored location history associated with attendance
  • Notification preferences

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, security requirements, fraud prevention, or legitimate business obligations.

Deletion Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. Once completed, the account and associated personal data will be permanently removed, except where retention is legally required.

Need Help?

If you experience any issues while requesting account deletion, please contact us at support@attendify.com.
